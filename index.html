MISSION

Our mission is to simplify scientific concepts about astronomy and astrophysics, share the latest space news and create engaging and fun content to give teens who love space the opportunity to learn, enjoy science and give them a chance to practice what they are passionate about.

Who Are We?

Astroteen magazine is an international virtual magazine launched in March 2025. We are a youth-led magazine focused on everything related to space and space technology — made by teens, for teens. It's a safe place for teens who are passionate about space to grow their love, learn more, and connect with peers who share the same passion.

MESSAGE FROM OUR FOUNDER

What inspired me to start this magazine is that while many teens like me are very passionate about space science and astronomy not all of them have the access to dive deeper in this field due to the lack of resources and opportunities so I decided to build a space for teenagers to learn more, foster their skills and have the chance to reach their goals.
